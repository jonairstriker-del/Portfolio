# Overview

The Jonair Portfolio Website is a modern, responsive, multi-page portfolio website designed to showcase my journey as an aspiring UI/UX Designer and Creative Editor.

The website serves as a professional platform where visitors, recruiters, and potential clients can learn about my background, skills, certifications, experience, and creative projects. It highlights both my design capabilities and editing expertise through a visually appealing and user-friendly interface.

The overall design follows a premium dark-themed aesthetic inspired by modern portfolio websites, utilizing glassmorphism effects, smooth animations, and a clean layout to create an engaging user experience.

---

# Purpose

The purpose of this website is to:

* Establish a professional online presence.
* Showcase UI/UX design projects and creative work.
* Display certifications and achievements.
* Highlight skills, tools, and experience.
* Demonstrate design thinking and creativity.
